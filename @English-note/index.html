M5U1 word using

  1. betray
    v.
    ——feel betrayed by 感觉被…背叛
    ——betray A to B (eg. betray one’s companion to the opponent 向对手出卖自己的同伴)
    (eg. He was afraid of something that would made him betray himself.他害怕会有什么东西使他背叛自己。)

  2. pretend
    v.
    ——pretend to do / to be done
    ——pretend to be doing (eg. He pretended to be reading when teacher came in. 他在老师进来时假装在读书。)
    ——pretend to have done / to have been done (eg. pretend to have known the truth 假装已经知道真相)
    (eg. He admitted pretend to be reading when the teacher came in.)
    非谓语的2/1/2结构(现在、进行、过去)

  3. admit
    v.
    ——admit sth 承认做某事(eg. admit my mistake承认我的错误)
    ——admit one’s doing (one’s可用宾格代替) (eg. admit having done)
    ——be admitted into / gain admission to/into a famous university 进入、加入

  4. beg
    v.
    ——I beg you pardon. 请再说一遍。
    ——The millionaire ended up begging. 那个百万富翁沦落到去乞讨。
    ——turn/drive away the beggar 赶走那个乞丐

  5. sware
    v.
    (eg. He swore that he would nener forgive his companion for his betrayal.他发誓决不原谅他的同伴对他的背叛。)

  6. focus
    n.焦点
    ——(eg. become the focus of the public attention成为公众关注的焦点)
    v.集中注意力于
    ——focus/concentrate/fix (one’s attention) on
    ——with one’s attention focused/concentrated/fixed on
    ——keep/stay focused on
    类比：
    ——pay one’s attention to
    ——with one’s attention paid to

  7. keep pace with
    ——keep pace with = keep up with 与…保持一致
    ——catch up with 追赶上

  8. manner
    n.
    ——in this/that manner（方式）
    ——table manner(举止)(eg. It’s good manners to do… 做…是有修养的)

  9. guilty
    adj.
    ——be guilty about 对…内疚 (eg. be guilty about one’s cruel remarks)
    ——be guilty of 犯…罪 (eg. be guilty of piracy盗版)

  10. cruel
    adj.
    ——be cruel to 对…残忍

  11. dislike
    ——dislike to do(单次、具体的行为)
    ——dislike doing(长期、习惯的行为)
    句式：
    ——dislike接宾语从句时强制加 it. 常用：dislike it when
    (eg. I dislike it when people talk with their mouth full.我不喜欢人们嘴里塞满东西说话。)

  12. argue
    v.
    ——argue with sb about sth. 与某人争论某事
    n.
    ——have an argument/arguments with sb about sth. 与某人争论某事

  13. apologize
    v.
    ——apologize to sb about sth 就某事向某人道歉
    n.
    ——make an apology to sb about sth 就某事向某人道歉

  14. quarrel
    v.
    ——quarrel with sb about sth 因某事与某人争吵
    n.
    ——eg. A quarrel between the couple broke out. 夫妻间的争吵爆发了。

  15. sensitive
    adj.
    ——be sensitive to sth 对…敏感
    root.
    ——sb make sense of sth 某人理解某物
    ——sth make sense 某物有意义
    ——it doesn’t make any sense/makes no sense to do 做…没有意义
    ——There’s no sense in doing sth.
    类比：
    ——There’s no need to do sth.
    ——It’s no use/good doing sth.

  16. envy
    n.
    ——do sth out of envy/respect/kindness 出于…(嫉妒、尊重、善意)做…
    (eg. make cruel remarks on sth out of envy 出于嫉妒对某事发表残酷的评论)
    v.
    ——envy sb sth
    (eg. envy the athlete his talent 羡慕那个运动员的天赋)

  17. agree
    v.
    ——agree with sth/名词性从句
    ——agree with(vi. 适应天气水土)
    ——agree to 主要用来表示一方提出一项建议、安排、计划等，另一方同意协作
    ——agree on 多方达成共识

  18. before long
    ——before long (作状语，很快、不久)
    ——long before + 宾语 ( sb do sth ) 很久以前

  19. hopeless
    adj.
    ——be hopeless about 对…感到无望

  20. acquaintance
    n.
    ——make the acquaintance of sb 结识某人
    ——I and he was noding acquaintance.(点头之交)

  21. attitude
    n.
    ——have / hold / take an [active(积极的)/passive(消极的)] attitude to 对…持有一个…的态度

  22. anchor
    v.
    ——be anchored in 扎根于，使基于
    (eg. Art is anchored in life.艺术是扎根于生活的。)

  23. cautious
    adj.
    ——be cautious about 对…小心谨慎
    n.
    ——do sth with caution 小心谨慎地做某事

  24. base
    ——be based on / on the basic of
    ——Based on sth , … / Basing sth on sth , …
    (eg. Basing his investigation(调查) on his assumption(假设) ， the detecitive failed to find the truth. 把他的调查建立在他的假设上 , 那个侦探没能发现真相。)
    (eg. On the basic of his research , the scientist draw the conclusion that… 根据他的研究，科学家得出这样的结论)
    (eg. We need the fact on which to base our thinking. 我们需要事实作为我们思考的基础。)
    (eg. The judge needs convincing evidence on which to base his sentence. 法官需要有令人信服的证据作为量刑的基础。)

  25. eager
    ——hope / wish / long / starve for sth 渴望某物
    ——be eager / dying / desprate / anxious for sth / to do sth
    ——have a desire to sth

  26. respond
    v.
    ——sb respond to sth 对某事作出反应
    n.
    ——do A in response to B 做A来回应B

  27. end up
    ——end up + adj. (eg. end up amazed)
    ——end up + doing (eg. end up begging 沦落到乞讨)
    ——end up + prep. (eg. end up in failure / in prison 以失败/坐牢告终)

  28. rely
    v.
    ——rely / count / depend on 依赖于
    ——rely on it that 信得过

  29. committed
    adj.
    ——be committed to doing sth 致力于做某事
    ——be committed to his motherland 忠于他的祖国
    n.
    ——make a commitment to do sth 承诺做某事

  30. mercy
    n.
    ——show mercy to = have mercy on 对…怜悯
    ——at the mercy of 受…控制

  31. blame
    n.
    ——take the blame for sth 为某事承担责任
    v.
    ——I don’t blame you. 我不责怪你。
    ——blame sb for sth = blame sth on sb 把某事归咎于某人
    句式：
    ——sb be blamed for… 某人因某事而受到责备（已发生）
    ——sb be to blame for sth 某人应该因某事而受到责备（未发生）
    ——It’s I that am to blame. 该受责备的是我。

  32. gifted
    adj.
    ——be gifted at 对某事有天赋
    n.
    ——have a gift for 对某事有天赋

  33. shift
    v.
    ——shift the responsibility onto sb 把责任转嫁到某人身上
    n.
    ——night shit 夜班

M5U2 word-using

  1. economy
    n.
    ——(eg. The economy is picking up.经济有所好转。)

  2. consultant
    n.
    ——(eg. turn to the consultant for some consultations 向顾问咨询一些问题)
    v.
    ——consult sb about sth 就…咨询某人
    ——consult with sb 和某人磋商

  3. debate
    v.
    ——debate with sb about sth 和某人辩论某事
    ——(eg. the topic is under debate = the topic is being debated)
    n.
    ——set off a heated debate 引起了一场激烈的争论

  4. open the floor
    ——give / take / open + the floor (发言权)

  5. cut back on
    ——cut back on = cut down on
    ——cut sth into pieces 把某物切成两半/碎片
    ——cut sth in half 把…砍成两半
    ——cut up 切丁
    ——cut off the internet 切断
    ——cut away the cancer 切除
    ——cut sth out of sth 剪裁出
    ——cut down the tree 砍倒

  6. greedy
    adj.
    ——be greedy for 对…贪婪

  7. responsibility
    n.
    ——bear / take / have / hide from the responsibility ( for sth / to do sth ) 承担/逃避责任做某事
    adj.
    ——be responsible / irresponsible for sth 对某事负责/不负责

  8. belief
    n.
    ——(eg. I hold a firm belief that 我坚信…)

  9. operate
    v.
    ——the machine is operating in problem 机器运转有问题
    ——operate a company 经营一个公司
    ——operate on a patient 给病人动手术

  10. quantity
    n.
    ——a large quantity of + ( n.[c]+V复 ) / ( n.[u]+V单 ) 大量的
    ——large quantities of+ ( n.+v复 ) 大量的
    ——(eg. purchase raw material in large quantities 大量购买原材料 )

  11. willing
    n.
    ——do sth at a will 任性地做某事

  12. use up / run out of
    use up
    ——Human beings are using up nature resource ( 主动 )
    ——sth be used up ( 被动 )
    run out
    ——sth run out ( 只有主动 )
    run out of
    ——sb run out of sth
    give out
    ——my legs give out 我的腿没有力气了、用尽了 ( 主动 )

  13. arrival
    ——…就…
    ——Upon+n./doing = on+n./doing = as soon as
    一The moment/
    ——No sooner had sb done sth than sb did / when sb did 某人刚做某事，某人就做了某事

  14. arrest
    v.
    ——arrest sb tor sth 因为某事逮捕某人
    ——sb be under arrest 某人被逮捕

  15. conflict
    v.
    ——the reality conflicts with ones dream 与…冲突
    n.
    ——the reality is in conflict with one’s dream
    ——the conflict over boundary issue 边界问题的冲突
    ——the conflict between A and B A与B之间的冲突

  16. desertification
    n.
    ——(eg. better administration of areas that are likely to turn into desert 更好地管理可能变成沙漠的地区)

  17. measure
    v.
    一book a suite measuring 200 m2 预订一个200平方米的套房
    ——measure one’s success in terms of one’s grades / academic achievements 用某人的成绩衡量成功
    n.
    ——It is tine that effective measures were taken to do sth 是时候采取了有效的措施去做某事

  18. flood
    n.
    ——a flood of refugees 大量的、汹涌而入的难民
    ——bitter memories came flooding back 痛苦的回忆涌上心头

  19. range
    n.
    ——within the range of 在…的射程内
    ——a wide range of 大范围的
    ——the local grocery store (杂货铺) provide a lot of daily items (日常生活用品), ranging from shampoo to gloves. 从洗发水到手套应有尽有

  20. under way ＝ in process

  21. appreciate
    n.
    ——a real appreciate of your help 非常感谢你的帮助
    v.
    ——appreciate doing / one’s doing 欣赏、感谢做某事

    ——I really appreciate it if you could give me an immediate response.

  22. let off = give off 散发出

  23. fuel

  24. consume

    adj.
    ——be absorbed in 专心致志于…

  26. do one’s part
