M5U1

wordlist

  1. betray / betrayed / betrayed——(v.) 出卖 | (n.) 背叛 betrayal

  2. cheerful——(adj.) 高兴的 = delighted = joyful = pleased

  3. admit / admitted / admitted——(v.) 承认 | (n.) 承认、允许进入admission

  4. beg / begged / begging——(v.) 请求，恳求；乞讨 | (n.) beggar

  5. swear / swore / sworn——(v.) 发誓

  6. sincerely——(adv.) 真诚地 | (n.) 真诚 sincerity

  7. focus——(v.) 集中注意力于 = concentrate = fix

  8. horrible——(adj.) 极坏的 | (n.) 恐怖horror

  9. guilty——(adj.) 内疚的 | (n.) 罪guilt | ant.无辜的innocent | (n.) 无辜innocence

  10. cruel——(adj.) 残忍的 | (n.) 残忍cruelty

  11. remark——(n.) 评论 = comment

  12. argue——(v.) 争吵 | (n.) 争吵argument（唯一一个去e的）

  13. awkward——(adj.) 尴尬的 = embarrass；笨拙的 = clumsy

  14. apologize——(v.) 道歉 | (n.) 道歉apology

  15. sensitive——(adj.) 敏感的 | (root.) 感受、意义sense | (adj.) 理智的sensible | (n.) 体贴sensitivity

  16. athletic——(adj.) 健壮的 | (n.) 运动员athlete

  17. envy——(v.) &(n.) 嫉妒 | (adj.) 嫉妒的envious = jealous | (n.) 嫉妒jealousy

  18. gifted——(adj.) 有天赋的 = talented | (n.) 礼物、天赋gift = talent

  19. disagreement——(n.) 分歧 | (v.) 不同意disagree

  20. identity——(n.) 身份 | (v.) 识别identify | (n.) 辨认identification

  21. amusement——(n.) 娱乐 | (adj.) 愉快的amused | (adj.) 有趣的amusing

  22. hopeless——(adj.) 无望的 = (n.) despair = (adj) desperate | (ant.) 充满希望的hopeful

  23. overcome——(v.) 克服 = get over

  24. acquaintance——(n.) 熟人 | (v.) acquaint

  25. cautious——(adj.) 小心的 | (n.) 小心caution

  26. eager——(adj.) 亟不可待的 = dying = anxious =desperate

  27. adolescent——(adj.) 青春期的 | (n.) 青春期adolescence

  28. respond——(v.) 回应 | (n.) 回应response

  29. security——(n.) 安全 | (adj.) 安全的secure | (adj.) 使安全的secured

  30. rely——(v.) 依赖 | (adj.) 可靠的reliable | (adj.) 不独立的reliant | (n.) 依赖reliance

  31. committed——(adj.) 尽心尽力的 | (v.) 犯罪commit | (n.) 承诺commitment

  32. mercy——(n.) 仁慈 | (adj.) 仁慈的merciful | (adj.) 无情的merciless

word using

  1. betray
    v.
    ——feel betrayed by 感觉被…背叛
    ——betray A to B (eg. betray one’s companion to the opponent 向对手出卖自己的同伴)
    (eg. He was afraid of something that would made him betray himself.)

  2. pretend
    v.
    ——pretend to do / to be done
    ——pretend to be doing (eg. He pretended to be reading when teacher came in. 他在老师进来时假装在读书。)
    ——pretend to have done / to have been done (eg. pretend to have known the truth 假装已经知道真相)
    (eg. He admitted pretend to be reading when the teacher came in.)
    非谓语的2/1/2结构(现在、进行、过去)

  3. admit
    v.
    ——admit sth (eg. admit my mistake)
    ——admit one’s doing (one’s可用宾格代替) (eg. admit having done)
    ——be admitted into / gain admission to/into a famous university 进入、加入

  4. beg
    v.
    ——I beg you pardon. 请再说一遍。
    ——The millionaire ended up begging. 那个百万富翁沦落到去乞讨。
    ——turn/drive away the beggar 赶走那个乞丐

  5. sware
    v.
    (eg. He swore that he would nener forgive his companion for his betrayal.)

  6. focus
    n.焦点
    ——(eg. become the focus of the public attention)
    v.集中注意力于
    ——focus/concentrate/fix (one’s attention) on
    ——with one’s attention focused/concentrated/fixed on
    ——keep/stay focused on
    类比：
    ——pay one’s attention to
    ——with one’s attention paid to

  7. keep pace with
    ——keep pace with = keep up with 与…保持一致
    ——catch up with 追赶上

  8. manner
    n.
    ——in this/that manner（方式）
    ——table manner(举止)(eg. It’s good manners to do… 做…是有修养的)

  9. guilty
    adj.
    ——be guilty about 对…内疚 (eg. be guilty about one’s cruel remarks)
    ——be guilty of 犯…罪 (eg. be guilty of piracy盗版)

  10. cruel
    adj.
    ——be cruel to 对…残忍

  11. dislike
    ——dislike to do(单次、具体的行为)
    ——dislike doing(长期、习惯的行为)
    句式：
    ——dislike接宾语从句时强制加 it. 常用：dislike it when
    (eg. I dislike it when people talk with their mouth full.)

  12. argue
    v.
    ——argue with sb about sth.
    n.
    ——have an argument/arguments with sb about sth.

  13. apologize
    v.
    ——apologize to sb about sth
    ——make an apology to sb about sth

  14. quarrel
    v.
    ——quarrel with sb about sth
    n.
    ——eg. A quarrel between the couple broke out. 夫妻间的争吵爆发了。

  15. sensitive
    adj.
    ——be sensitive to sth 对…敏感
    root.
    ——sb make sense of sth 某人理解某物
    ——sth make sense 某物有意义
    ——it doesn’t make any sense/makes no sense to do 做…没有意义
    ——There’s no sense in doing sth.
    类比：
    ——There’s no need to do sth.
    ——It’s no use/good doing sth.

  16. envy
    n.
    ——do sth out of envy/respect/kindness 出于…(嫉妒、尊重、善意)做…
    (eg. make cruel remarks on sth out of envy)
    v.
    ——envy sb sth
    (eg. envy the athlete his talent)

  17. agree
    v.
    ——agree with sth/名词性从句
    ——agree with(vi. 适应天气水土)
    ——agree to 主要用来表示一方提出一项建议、安排、计划等，另一方同意协作
    ——agree on 多方达成共识

  18. before long
    ——before long (作状语，很快、不久)
    ——long before + 宾语 ( sb do sth ) 很久以前

  19. hopeless
    adj.
    ——be hopeless about 对…感到无望

  20. acquaintance
    n.
    ——make the acquaintance of sb 结识某人
    ——I and he was noding acquaintance.(点头之交)

  21. attitude
    n.
    ——have / hold / take an [active(积极的)/passive(消极的)] attitude to 对…持有一个…的态度

  22. anchor
    v.
    ——be anchored in 扎根于，使基于
    (eg. Art is anchored in life.艺术是扎根于生活的。)

  23. cautious
    adj.
    ——be cautious about 对…小心谨慎
    n.
    ——do sth with caution 小心谨慎地做某事

  24. base
    ——be based on / on the basic of
    ——Based on sth , … / Basing sth on sth , …
    (eg. Basing his investigation(调查) on his assumption(假设) ， the detecitive failed to find the truth. 把他的调查建立在他的假设上 , 那个侦探没能发现真相。)
    (eg. On the basic of his research , the scientist draw the conclusion that… 根据他的研究，科学家得出这样的结论)
    (eg. We need the fact on which to base our thinking. 我们需要事实作为我们思考的基础。)
    (eg. The judge needs convincing evidence on which to base his sentence. 法官需要有令人信服的证据作为量刑的基础。)

  25. eager
    ——hope / wish / long / starve for sth 渴望某物
    ——be eager / dying / desprate / anxious for sth / to do sth
    ——have a desire to sth

  26. respond
    v.
    ——sb respond to sth
    n.
    ——do A in response to B 做A来回应B

  27. end up
    ——end up + adj. (eg. end up amazed)
    ——end up + doing (eg. end up begging)
    ——end up + prep. (eg. end up in failure / in prison)

  28. rely
    v.
    ——rely / count / depend on 依赖于
    ——rely on it that 信得过

  29. committed
    adj.
    ——be committed to doing sth 致力于做某事
    ——be committed to his motherland 忠于他的祖国
    n.
    ——make a commitment to do sth 承诺做某事

  30. mercy
    n.
    ——show mercy to = have mercy on 对…怜悯
    ——at the mercy of 受…控制

  31. blame
    n.
    ——take the blame for sth 为某事承担责任
    v.
    ——I don’t blame you. 我不责怪你。
    ——blame sb for sth = blame sth on sb 把某事归咎于某人
    句式：
    ——sb be blamed for… 某人因某事而受到责备（已发生）
    ——sb be to blame for sth 某人应该因某事而受到责备（未发生）
    ——It’s I that am to blame. 该受责备的是我。

  32. gifted
    adj.
    ——be gifted at 对某事有天赋
    n.
    ——have a gift for 对某事有天赋

  33. shift
    v.
    ——shift the responsibility onto sb 把责任转嫁到某人身上
    n.
    ——night shit 夜班

  4. open the floor——自由发言 | floor (n.) 发言权、话语权 | take the Hloor 接过发言权

  5. flow/flowed/flowed——(v.) 流 (n.) 流动 | fly/flew/flown

  6. Produition——n.产量、生产 | produce (v.) 生产/(n.) 农产品 | product (n.) (工场里的）产品 |　productive (adj.) 高产的

  7. greedy——(adj.) 贪婪的 | greed (n.) 贪婪

  8. responsibity——(n.) 责任 | responsible (adj.) 有责任的、负责任的 | irresponsible (ant.) 不负责的

  9. environmental——(adj.) 环境的 | environment (n.) 环境 | user/environmentally-frenly (adj.) 对用户友好的/环保的

  10. quantity——(n.) 数量 | quality (n.) 质量

  11. credit——(n.) 赞场 (＝praise) 、信用、学分 | (eg.) buy sth on credit 赊帐

  12. operate——(v.) 经营、动手术 | operator (n.) 人 | operation (n.) 操作

  13. raw——(adj.) 原始的，未经加工处理的 | (eg.) raw fish 生鱼片

  14. willing——(adj.) 愿意的 | willingness (n.) 愿意 | will (n.) 意志、意愿
